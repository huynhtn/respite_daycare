about

Owner and operator, Kris Mason is a Licensed Nurse, with 30 years of experience working with seniors in independent, assisted living and memory care communities. Her mother will tell you she knew at the age of 5 years old that she wanted to work with seniors. She and her husband purchased a large home on a 5 acre farm and asked if her parents would join them on the Family Farm.

As a professional in the corporate world of long-term care, she wanted to develop a different and better option for seniors and their working spouses and children. She wanted to provide them an option that allowed them to have their loved ones stay at a home longer while giving them an engaging social environment, so that seniors would not need to be left at home, isolated, and unattended during 8-10 hours of work days.

The Maple Falls senior is so under-served with a lack of affordable resources for seniors and their families. Kris's husband, Ed, also a professional in longer care, her parents, Richard and Kathleen and her work together to create a loving, supportive, safe and fun space for seniors. And that is how Echo Valley Adult Daycare and Respite came to be. Our family caring for your family.
