FAQ

1. What are the rates for adults day care?

- Rates start at just $15 per hour

2. What are the hours for adult day care?

- Typical hours are 8AM-8PM Monday through Friday

- Additional times and hours may be available.

3. What are the rates for respite stay?

- Respite stays starts at $250 per day. This will be determined by the amount of care and supervision your loved one needs.

4.How long is respite stay?

- A respite stay can be overnight stay, a few days to a few weeks, or an extended stay.

5. What is an Adult Day Care Home vs. Adult Family Home?

- Basically, Adult Family Homes are houses where people who need care live full time with resident caregivers. Adult Day Care Homes, like Echo Valley Adult Day Care, are homes where those who need care go for short periods of time during the day, so that their family members can continue to work, get a break from caregiving, or just take time away for themselves. This allows the family to keep their loved one at home longer. We like to think of ourselves as a short term solution to a problem!

6. Does insurance pay for daycare or respite?

- At this time insurance does not cover this expense.

7. Do you provide transportation to and from your home?

- At this time we can help provide transportation for some of our guests Please ask if this service is available for your loved one
